# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 8, 2026**

## Overview

This privacy policy applies to our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or process any personal user data.** Our application operates entirely on your device without transmitting any information to external servers.

### What This Means

- No personal information is collected
- No usage analytics or telemetry data is collected
- No device identifiers are logged
- No cookies or tracking technologies are used
- No data is shared with third parties
- No accounts or login systems exist

## App Functionality

Our application functions locally on your device. All data processing occurs on your device, and no information leaves your device.

## Permissions

If the application requests any device permissions (such as camera, location, etc.), these are used solely for the specific feature you are utilizing and no data from these permissions is collected or transmitted.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this privacy policy.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at the repository issues page on GitHub.
